WHAT IS THE SEP PROGRAM?

The Software Engineering Pilot (SEP) is a computer science and software engineering class for students from grades 6 to 12. The program curriculum varies from computer programming, robotics, web design, mobile development, physical computing, mobile computing and SO MUCH MORE.
It allows for students to develop problem-solving skills, collaborate with others, build up leadership skills, prepare for the outside world and show off their creativity.
